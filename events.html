Events

Women Situation Room is a movement with the goal of eliminating obstacles to women’s participation in electoral processes, especially violence against women in all its forms, which has been identified as a major obstacle to women’s involvement in political affairs.

From 4th to 11th December 2016 a physical situation room was putted up in the Holiday Inn hotel Accra-Ghana to to help make Ghana's presidential election violence free with the help of the Ghanaian women and youth. Mafubo Ghana was there to help since our aim is to help eradicate women violence in Africa and its women that suffer most during election related violence.
