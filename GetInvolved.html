Get Involved

Information on the different ways to support the work of Mafubo

Commit to our side in the association
Become members or volunteers
Join a local team (see Network)
Create a national network
Providing us with material for our local centers (houses or trade schools)
Donating resources for specific actions

All donations will be detailed and monitored. As an organisation, we strive to let you know where and how we have been helped. Even though our resources are limited, we believe that by contributing to our funding, you would have helped our team who are full of ideas implement them to help empower a woman.

Thank you for your support!
