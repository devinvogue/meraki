Improving Living Conditions of African Families
through their main pillar: Women


Mafubo wants to accompany the women who live in extreme poverty and insecurity towards full empowerment and independence. To support them is to help an entire family and eventually an entire continent and the world.

Would you like to help us?

You can help us:

  • by committing to our side in the association. Become members or volunteers! Join a local team (see Network) or create a national network.
  • by providing us with material for our local centers (houses or trade schools) or for specific actions: Household equipment, household appliances, computer equipment, sewing machines, carpentry materials, etc.
  • All donations will be detailed monitoring, we strive to let you know where and how they helped. contributing to our funding. Our teams are full of ideas to help African women but our resources are limited.
