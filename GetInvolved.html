Get Involved

Information on the different ways to support the work of Mafubo!

You can help us:

By committing to our side in the association. Become members or volunteers! Join a local team (see Network) or create a national network. By providing us with material for our local centers (houses or trade schools) or for specific actions:
  1. Household equipment
  2. Household appliances
  3. Computer equipment
  4. Sewing machines
  5. Carpentry materials
All donations will be detailed monitoring, we strive to let you know where and how they helped. Contributing to our funding. Our teams are full of ideas to help African women but our resources are limited.

Thank you for your support
