IMPROVING LIVING CONDITIONS OF AFRICAN FAMILIES
THROUGH THEIR MAIN PILLAR:WOMEN.


Woman is the guardian of humanity, and children are its future. Yet women make up the largest group suffering the most from extreme poverty; they are the most vulnerable group in society. Their children bear the negative consequences throughout their lives. In the popular neighbourhoods of African cities, in the townships and slums, many women live in unacceptable circumstances within their household, because they think they do not have a choice, because they do not know where else to go and how to shelter their children. Their fighting spirit, tenacity and resourcefulness to keep their heads above water command respect. This is why Mafubo wants to accompany the women who live in extreme poverty and insecurity towards full empowerment and independence.

To support them is to help an entire family and eventually an entire continent and the world.

Would you like to help us?

Contact us for more information on the different ways to support the work of Mafubo! You can help us:
  • by committing to our side in the association. Become members or volunteers! Join a local team (see Network) or create a national network.
  • by providing us with material for our local centers (houses or trade schools) or for specific actions: Household equipment, household appliances, computer equipment, sewing machines, carpentry materials, etc.
  • All donations will be detailed monitoring, we strive to let you know where and how they helped. contributing to our funding. Our teams are full of ideas to help African women but our resources are limited.
