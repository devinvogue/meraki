Service Overview

  • Empower African women through financial independence and access to decent housing.
  • Help protect women against violence in urban areas (rape, domestic violence, physical abuse, etc,)

  • Facilitate access to health care for the prevention and cure of diseases of the reproductive system (cancer, post-birth trauma, etc.)

  • Promote equal opportunities in education for underprivileged girls, ensuring they live in the basic conditions for success.

    • Mission


    Mafubo fights extreme poverty, insecurity and the various forms of violence against women in Africa and elsewhere. Mafubo wants to ensure that girls from disadvantaged backgrounds are guaranteed conditions for academic success

    Vision


    A world where all women are safe, autonomous and empowered.
